Burrito

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Torilla
  • Green Pepper
  • Onion

How to prepare!

  1. Cook the chicken breast until it is white and add any seasoning.
  2. Cut onions and green peppers.
  3. Cook all together for about 5 minutes.
  4. Lay out torrtilla and add in your ingridients.
  5. EAT!
